About Me


Hi I'm Josh. I'm a former academic turned software engineer.


For the past six years I collaborated with developers, designers, and data scientists as a scholar-in-residence at a software company that specializes in academic software. Although I love the rigor of research and the critical thinking of academic work, I was ready for a new challenge. So, I exchanged the ivory tower for zeros and ones, and I haven't looked back. I'm passionate about providing eloquent solutions to complex problems, and writing software that improves the way we live and experience the world.


I'm proficient in Python, JavaScript, CSS3, and HTML5, among others. I love drinking coffee and learning new things. I believe that collaboration is critical for quality software.
